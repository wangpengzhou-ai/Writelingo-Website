Spot a pattern in your writing? Writelingo turns it into a quiz built just for you.

Fill in the blank · 冗余 Q1 / 12
Could you this document when you have time?
✓ Nice — that's right

Discuss is transitive here, so it takes a direct object without about.

Could you discuss this document with me?
discuss 后直接接讨论的内容，不加 about。
Fill in the blank · 形容词词尾 Q2 / 12
I'm really in this role.
✓ Nice — that's right

-ed adjectives describe how you feel. -ing adjectives describe what causes the feeling.

The meeting was interesting. I was really interested.
-ing 表示引发感受的事物，-ed 表示你本人的感受。
Pick the fix · 冠词 Q4 / 12
Let's schedule quick sync after lunch.
✓ Nice — that's right

Use a before quick sync because it names one countable meeting.

Let's schedule a quick sync after lunch.
sync 是可数名词，这里表示“一次简短同步”。
Fill in the blank · 介词 Q3 / 12
She's very good managing teams.
✕ Not quite

Use good at for skills or abilities. Good in usually points to a field or situation.

She's very good at managing teams.
表示“擅长做某事”时用 good at。
